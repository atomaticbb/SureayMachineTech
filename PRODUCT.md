# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
International OEM buyers and procurement engineers searching for reliable industrial blades and replacement parts. They usually compare suppliers quickly on mobile and desktop, then request quotation with technical specs.

## Product Purpose
Sureay's website proves manufacturing capability, technical credibility, and response speed for industrial blade sourcing. Success means more qualified RFQ submissions and catalog downloads from global buyers.

## Brand Personality
Professional, direct, engineering-driven. Tone is precise and confident, focused on factory strength and customization rather than lifestyle language.

## Anti-references
Do not look like generic SaaS dashboards, pastel startup templates, or over-decorated consumer ecommerce pages. Avoid playful gradients, rounded bubble UI, and marketing fluff.

## Design Principles
1. Put technical clarity before decoration.
2. Lead every page toward RFQ conversion.
3. Show manufacturing credibility through concrete details.
4. Keep layouts robust, high-contrast, and easy to scan.
5. Prioritize quick trust signals for first-time global buyers.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard accessibility for key navigation and forms. Keep motion subtle and optional, preserve readable typography on mobile, and avoid color-only state communication.
